Executive Summary
PHARMA-AI is a full-stack web platform that transforms pharmaceutical formulation science by delivering comprehensive drug analysis in under 30 seconds. Given a SMILES string (the universal chemical language), it returns AI-powered predictions for solubility, excipient selection, stability, and pharmacokinetics - with interactive 3D visualization and PDF reports.
The vision: Expand from a hackathon-grade prototype into a life-saving platform that pharmaceutical companies, researchers, and clinicians can rely on to accelerate drug development - cutting weeks of work into seconds.
